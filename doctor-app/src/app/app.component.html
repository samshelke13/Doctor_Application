Our Services

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG): A diagnostic tool used to assess the electrical and muscular functions of the heart.
  • 2D Echo & Colour Doppler: A sonography and color Doppler of the heart, useful in diagnosing heart-related ailments like heart attacks, hypertension, valve diseases, and congenital heart conditions.
  • Stress Test: A treadmill test that records ECGs to diagnose blockages in coronary arteries, useful for patients experiencing chest pain and breathlessness.
  • Holter Monitoring: A 24-hour continuous monitoring of heart rate and rhythm to detect irregular heartbeats.
  • Balloon Valvotomy: A minimally invasive procedure using a catheter to widen heart valves, inserted through an artery in the groin or arm.
  • Pacemaker: A device used to regulate heart rhythm for patients with arrhythmia or heart rhythm disorders.
