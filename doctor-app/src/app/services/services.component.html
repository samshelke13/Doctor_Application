Our Services

2D Echo & Colour Droppler

This is basically a sonography and color Doppler of the heart. This test is useful in diagnosing heart related ailments like fresh/ previous heart attack, hypertension, valve related disease, congential heart disease etc.
Stress Test

This is simple test where patients are made to walk on a treadmill and ECGs are recorded simultaneously, this is indicated in patients with chest pain and breathlessness, to diagnose possibility of blockages in the coronary arteries of the heart.
Holter Monitoring

This is a machine wherein the heart rate and rhythm is continuously monitored over 24 hours to find out problems with heart beats and rhythm.
Angiography

This is a diagnostic procedure gold standard test to rule out the exact percentage of blockages in coronary artery. A radio opaque dye is injected into the artery and few x-ray shootsare taken in this procedure.
Angioplasty

This is the process to treat heart blockages by expanding them with balloon followed by putting up stents at the place of blockage so as to relieve the patient of chest pain and also prevent further heart attacks.
Pacemaker

This is a procedure required in patients with decrease heart rate. A small device is fit into the body which controls the beating of the heart and prevents fainting from decreased heart rate and death.
Ambulatory BP Monitoring

ABPM is a relatively new technique for assessing a person’s blood pressure. ABPM allows a doctor to assess your blood pressure during your routine daily living, instead of when you are sitting nervously on the doctor’s examination table.
Strain Imaging

Electrocardiogram

The electrocardiogram (ECG or EKG) is a diagnostic tool that is routinely used to assess the electrical and muscular functions of the heart. While it is a relatively simple test to perform, the interpretation of the ECG tracing requires significant amounts of training.
Pediatric and Neonatal Echo

Echocardiograms are used in Pediatric Echocardiography which creates a moving picture of the heart using sound waves in a non-invasive test. This test is done to evaluate and diagnose many heart conditions
Dobutamine Stress Echocardiogram

This test is used to check for signs of heart disease and assess fitness for surgery in patients who are not able to do the conventional Treadmill/ Stress test due to problems in the knee joint or obesity or any other patient related issues.
Pre-Operative Cardiac Fitness

Pre Operative Cardiac Fitness is a medically supervised programme that helps improve the health and well-being of people who have heart problems. Rehab programmes include exercise training, education on the heart and healthy living, and counselling to reduce stress and help you return to an active life.
Balloon Valvotomy

A balloon valvotomy is a minimally invasive procedure. A doctor uses a thin flexible tube (catheter) that is inserted through an artery in the groin or arm and threaded into the heart. When the tube reaches the narrowed mitral valve, a balloon device located on the tip of the catheter is quickly inflated. The narrowed or fused mitral valve leaflets are separated and stretched open as the balloon presses against them. This process enhances the functioning of Mitral valve and insures good flow of blood from left auricle to left ventricle.
